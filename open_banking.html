Fast and Easy Way Of Accepting Online Payments

What is Open Banking

Services that provide instant payments through online banking applications.

Thanks to the revised Payment Services Directive (PSD2) and Open Banking, LetKnow Pay is now able to connect merchants to than 6,000 banks in Europe in order to provide an instant, convenient and secure method for accepting payments.

0.3% processing fee;

No Chargebacks;

Instant Refunds and Settlements;

Decreased Number of Frauds;

Quick Integration;

Accepting over 6,000 banks in EU;

24/7 customer support.

How it works for customers

Whether shopping from a mobile device or desktop computer, purchases are completed in 4 simple steps without exposing sensitive credit card data. Shopping online has never been easier or safer than with Open Banking.

From mobile device

1

On the checkout page client selects pay by “banking app”;

2

Client Selects their bank from the list;

3

Redirected to app;

4

Approve transaction;

Purchase completed!

From desktop

1

On the checkout page client selects pay by “banking app”;

2

Client Selects their bank from the list;

3

Client enters telephone number linked to its bank;

4

Enter OTP number from SMS;

Purchase completed!
