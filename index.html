Custom Gateway

LetKnow Pay is a tailor-made payment service provider facilitating merchants to accept digital currency payments.

Our gateway offers you to accept multiple digital currencies with built-in exchange service. We facilitate our clients with bank account opening to guarantee the settlements in fiat.

How it works

LetKnow provides two methods of API integration:

Redirecting your customer to the payment page and embedding the QR code in a checkout or deposit page.

API 2.0: redirecting to the payment page

1

Client initiates a deposit on the merchant's shopping site and gets redirected to the LetKnow Payment Page

2

Client sends crypto

3

After our Node detects the payment, transaction is immediately approved and the client is redirected back to the merchant's shop

API 3.0: embedding the QR code

Customize the checkout page and embed the QR code and the crypto address in your page.

Limits

€1K /MONTHLY

Merchant Customers can pay or deposit funds no AML/KYC required.

€15K /yearly

For purchases and/or deposits up to EUR 15,000 yearly, Merchant needs to collect the following information from its customers: Address, Citizenship, Residence, Copy Passport, Selfie.

€30K /yearly

For purchases and/or deposits up to EUR 30,000 yearly, Merchant needs to collect proof of address, origin of funds, Video/Audio session for high risk countries only.

€45K /yearly

For purchases and/or deposits up to EUR 45,000 yearly, Merchant needs to collect origin of funds, Video/Audio session for all countries.

Settlements

Once the balance of €2,500 is reached, our Backoffice team will automatically settle the funds to a Merchant bank account, unless otherwise agreed upon.
